# PRODUCT.md

## Working title

Retro Flatland Shooter

## Product summary

A fast-loading browser shooter inspired by early 3D arcade and home-computer aesthetics. The player navigates a mostly flat arena, reads direction from strong ground patterns and sparse geometric landmarks, and survives by shooting simple enemies or targets with a visible projectile weapon.

## Player fantasy

- I instantly understand the world
- I can orient myself from the floor and landmarks
- My weapon feels immediate and arcade-like
- The game starts fast and is playable within seconds
- Score chasing encourages one-more-run replayability

## Intended mood

- synthetic
- spare
- readable
- arcade
- slightly eerie but not horror
- retro without parody

## Core player verbs

- move
- look
- shoot
- dodge
- survive
- score
- restart

## MVP definition

The MVP includes:

- first-person movement
- flat arena with repeating ground pattern
- sparse landmark geometry
- one projectile weapon
- simple targets or enemies
- score and health
- game-over and restart loop
- minimal HUD
- retro-styled sound effects
- best-score persistence

## Non-goals for MVP

- story campaign
- inventory
- crafting
- realistic physics
- multiplayer
- progression trees
- large menus
- complex enemy AI
