# 05 — World and Level Spec

## Arena

- one large square flat arena
- visible ground pattern across most of the playable space
- soft boundary via fog plus hard world bounds

## Spawn rules

- player spawns near center
- enemy or target spawns occur outside a safe radius around player
- newly spawned threats should be readable before they can immediately damage the player

## Landmarks

- sparse
- memorable silhouettes
- distributed across sectors
- intended primarily for orientation, secondarily as cover or obstacle

Examples:
- pillar clusters
- isolated spheres
- box towers
- arch-like gates

## Visibility

- medium-to-far visibility shaped by fog
- targets should usually appear before they are in immediate threat range

## Progression

For MVP, progression is time-based and score-based rather than map-based.
