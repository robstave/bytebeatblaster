# 03 — Visual Style

## Style summary

Minimal, geometric, retro-3D, readable, synthetic.

## Geometry rules

- prefer boxes, spheres, cylinders, and simple custom primitives
- avoid detailed meshes in MVP
- silhouettes should be obvious at a distance

## Ground treatment

The ground is one of the most important orientation tools in the game.

Requirements:
- large flat plane
- repeating checker, stripe, or grid pattern
- visible at medium-to-far distance
- pattern should communicate movement direction

## Sky and horizon

- use a plain sky color or simple gradient feel
- avoid complex skies in MVP
- fog is used to compress the horizon and improve depth perception

## Fog

- required in MVP
- dense enough to suggest distance and world boundary
- not so dense that the player loses all situational awareness

## Lighting

- simple directional or hemispheric lighting
- readability first
- do not chase realism

## VFX

- minimal muzzle flash
- simple projectile glow or trail
- small hit flash
- restrained damage feedback

## HUD style

- minimal
- clean
- high contrast
- does not obscure the world
