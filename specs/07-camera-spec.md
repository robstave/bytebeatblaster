# 07 — Camera Spec

## Role

The camera is a core readability tool. The player should orient from the visible world, not from a minimap.

## Settings

- first-person camera
- moderate FOV
- low enough sensitivity defaults to be controllable
- pitch clamp to avoid extreme angles
- no cinematic transitions in MVP

## Feedback hooks

- subtle recoil kick on firing
- subtle camera shake on damage
- no excessive screen shake

## Constraints

- avoid clipping through important close geometry
- keep aiming intuitive and aligned with projectile origin expectations
