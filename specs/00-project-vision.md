# 00 — Project Vision

## Working title

Retro Flatland Shooter

## Elevator pitch

A browser-based retro 3D arcade shooter set on a mostly flat arena where the player reads direction from the ground pattern, horizon, fog, and sparse geometric landmarks. The player survives by shooting visible projectiles at simple enemies or targets while chasing a high score.

## Player fantasy

The player should feel instantly oriented, fast, and empowered. The world should feel open and sparse rather than cluttered. Movement and shooting should be more arcade than simulation.

## Inspirations

This project is inspired by early 3D and pseudo-3D playfields from the 1980s and early 1990s, especially games where ground patterns and distant landmarks acted as navigational cues.

## Platform

Desktop browser first.

## Session length

1 to 10 minutes per run for MVP.

## MVP scope

- immediate browser startup
- first-person play
- one arena
- one weapon
- one or two enemy/target types
- score attack loop
- simple audio and HUD

## Post-MVP ideas

- wave structure
- pickups
- multiple arenas
- stylized sky effects
- additional weapons
- leaderboard
- optional controller support

## Explicit non-goals

- open world
- realistic terrain
- story-driven campaign
- heavy simulation
- realistic weapon behavior
